About us

C3 Experiential Learning Private Limited, an IIT and IIM alumni initiative, takes the best and refines them to be even better. The focus here of experiential learning, as the name suggests, is on experience. Education is focused on going beyond the world of theories and concentrating on the practical experience of putting those theories into action. Industrial Revolution 4.0 requires professionals who are already experienced with the practical aspects of their fields when they began a professional career. We are solving this by bringing experiential learning to the base level of primary education. We focus on taking in young children who have just stepped into the educational world and build their base of knowledge on experiential learning.We, at C3 Experiential Learning Private Limited, are developing a world-class and first of its kind product and thus, we need extraordinarily skilled people with us to get on board.
